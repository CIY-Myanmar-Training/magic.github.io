Magical Potion Recipe

Follow these steps to create a magical potion that will make your wishes come true!

  1. Gather Ingredients:
    • Stardust
    • Unicorn Tears
    • Dragon Scales
  2. Prepare the Cauldron
  3. Add Ingredients to Cauldron:
    1. Sprinkle Stardust
    2. Drop Unicorn Tears
    3. Carefully place Dragon Scales
  4. Stir the Potion Clockwise 7 Times
  5. Chant the Magic Spell
  6. Wait for Potion to Bubble and Glow
  7. Pour Potion into a Crystal Vial
  8. Your Magical Potion is Ready! Make a Wish and Enjoy!
